Connect Google Ads, Meta, TikTok, LinkedIn, X, Snapchat, Amazon, Pinterest, and Mailchimp — all from one dashboard. Create campaigns with goals and budgets, push to every platform in one click, and track unified analytics daily. Each client gets isolated credentials and campaigns. No more switching between ad platforms — plan, launch, and measure everything in MAAFlo.
